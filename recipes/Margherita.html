Margherita

Main Page

Description

Margherita pizza is a simple Italian pizza known for its fresh and minimal ingredients. It has a soft tomato base, melted mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil leaves, giving it a light but rich flavor.

ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 220–250°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on top.
  4. Add slices of mozzarella cheese evenly.
  5. Bake for 8–12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese melts.
  6. Add fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil after baking.
