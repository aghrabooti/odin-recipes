spaghetti

Main Page

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish made with long thin noodles served with a flavorful tomato-based sauce. It’s simple, filling, and can be customized with herbs, meat, or vegetables.

ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add salt.
  2. Cook spaghetti until al dente, then drain.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce and season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  5. Simmer the sauce for a few minutes.
  6. Mix the cooked spaghetti with the sauce.
  7. Serve with Parmesan cheese on top
