Olive Dip

Main Page

Description

A creamy, savory dip made from chopped olives blended with cream cheese or yogurt, often flavored with garlic and lemon. It has a rich, salty taste and is usually served with bread, crackers, or vegetables.

ingredients

  • olives
  • cream cheese or yogurt
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. Chop liives finely.
  2. Add cream cheese or Greek yogurt.
  3. Mix in liive oil, garlic powder, and lemon juice.
  4. Stir everything until smooth and well combined.
  5. Taste and adjust salt or lemon if needed.
  6. Chill for 10–15 minutes (optional) and serve.
